# 🌾 Shivaji Good Poha

A modern, responsive, and professional website for **Shivaji Good Poha**, a premium poha manufacturing company based in Gujarat, India.

🔗 **Live Website:** https://shivaji-seven.vercel.app/

---

## 📌 About The Project

Shivaji Good Poha is a modern business website designed to showcase the company's premium poha products, manufacturing process, company story, recipes, gallery, and contact information.

The website focuses on:

- Premium visual design
- Responsive layouts
- Product showcase
- Recipe discovery
- Company information
- Customer inquiries
- Accessibility
- SEO optimization
- Performance optimization
